# Product positioning

## Audience brief

Bluff Call is for poker players who are curious about game theory but find solver
charts too abstract to learn from. It removes the gap between reading an optimal
frequency and recognizing the decision at the table. The core benefit is immediate,
exact feedback after every hand: play a decision, then see the solved move and the
frequency behind it.

## Name and promise

- **Display name:** Bluff Call
- **Tagline:** Play a hand. See the solved move.
- **Hero headline:** Learn the optimal move after every hand

The name stays because it is short, sayable, specific to poker, and points at the
decision the player is learning to judge. Copy should sound like a clear note from a
poker player who built a compact teaching game: direct, mathematically precise, and
free of casino hype.
